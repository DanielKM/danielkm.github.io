About Me

About Me

Five years ago I completed my master's of science in biology, studying the impacts of phosphorus in water on wetland plants. It was loads of fun and I learned a lot, but my science career really wasn't taking me in the direction I wanted. So, a year after graduating I quit my entry level science job and started learning programming and web development to switch into the tech field.

I am currently working as the VP of Engineering at an AI-powered start-up based in Waterloo, Ontario. Being as it is a small start-up, I wear many hats and have many roles and responsibilities. I get to see the entire lifecycle of the product - from talking to customers and recieving feedback, to taking part in high-level discussions about requested features and user experience and finally, overseeing the development team.

I enjoy providing good, clean code that other developers can use easily. I think good code is something that even a someone who can't program should be able to read and get a good sense of what is going on.

On a personal note, I love pretty much any form of adventure! I like to go backpacking, camping, biking and train in muay thai. I also like both playing and making video games!

My Projects

My Projects







































Summatti

Summatti is a one-stop shop for big businesses to bring their data to one place and get additional insights from AI and machine learning that they can't get anywhere else. As the VP of Engineering at Summatti, I helped develop this product from its' inception to being deployed with international businesses with billions of revenue.

Summatti provided excellent value to our customers because of the way we recieved feedback and converted that into amazing new features. We integrated with a variety of communication software and put all their data into one place for their ease of use. Summatti was built using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React and Node).



























Cambium Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS)

The purpose of this LIMS was to allow field technicians to input sample collection data in real time, alerting lab technicians that there was incoming soil and concrete samples.

It saved the company thousands of dollars per month, reduced errors, and increased the speed of their process by a factor of ten. We developed the application using vanilla Javascript, HTML, CSS, and PHP with a mySQL database.

Personality AI

The Personality AI is another project being worked on by MicroFluence where you can input your social media accounts and it will examine them using the IBM Watson AI. The artificial intelligence will then give you a score based off of your profile and that will be extrapolated into a social media personality.
